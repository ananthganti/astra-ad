Anomaly Detection Demo

This demo showcases Anomaly Detection for common daily-life anomalies. It is a Step-1 demonstration designed to provide an intuitive understanding of how anomaly detection works.

Future Improvements

The performance of this system can be significantly improved with:

  • Fine-tuning the model for a specific scene
  • Threshold tuning
  • Incremental / Adaptive Normality Modeling
  • RSM (Running Score Monitoring)

How to Use

  1. Select a demo video from the drop-down menu (top-left).
  2. The video player and corresponding anomaly score plot will appear.
  3. Press Play to start the video.
  4. Click anywhere on the video progress bar to jump to a specific time/frame.
  5. Click on "Prev Frame"/"Next Frame" to navigate frame by frame.
